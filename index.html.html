LBV Venture Academy for Business Development, Commercialization & Investor Readiness

10-week, 50-hour fully virtual program designed to help MA innovation-driven companies become investor-ready

Background

LBV Venture Academy is part of Commonwealth Corporation's Express Program and its Workforce Training Fund (WTF). This comprehensive training program is designed to help Massachusetts innovation-driven companies develop the skills and knowledge needed to become investor-ready and successfully commercialize their products.

Reimbursement Details

  • Up to $15,000 per year per employer
  • Up to $300 per hour of training
  • Up to $3,000 per person per course
  • Express Program can cover up to 100% of training costs for eligible employers

Context

LBV Venture Academy helps Massachusetts innovation-driven companies become investor-ready through a structured, progressive 5-module program. The curriculum leverages cutting-edge technologies including GenAI/AgenticAI, data science, and analytics, utilizing both proprietary and public databases to provide comprehensive market intelligence and strategic insights.

Each module builds upon the previous one, creating a comprehensive learning journey that covers everything from initial market assessment to final investor presentations and fundraising readiness.

Five Modules

Our comprehensive program consists of five progressive modules, each designed to build essential skills for business development and investor readiness:

Module 1: VENTURE & MARKET ASSESSMENT

C-Record: C-13924

This foundational module covers comprehensive market research, market opportunity sizing, the 5A's of Innovation & Entrepreneurship™ framework, and technology readiness levels (VRL/TRL). Learn to assess market potential, identify opportunities, and evaluate your venture's readiness for commercialization.

Learn More

Module 2: COMMERCIALIZATION-READINESS and GoTo Market Business Model

C-Record: C-14047

Focus on revenue generation strategies and the QFAR™ methodology (Quality, Feasibility, Attractiveness, Risk). Develop a comprehensive go-to-market business model that addresses market entry, customer acquisition, and sustainable revenue streams.

Learn More

Module 3: INVESTMENT STRATEGY & BUSINESS MODEL

C-Record: C-14048

Master business modeling techniques, explore various financing options, and understand capital structure through our proprietary capital structure grid/decoder. Learn to align your business model with investor expectations and optimize your funding strategy.

Learn More

Module 4: INVESTOR & COMMERCIALIZATION-READINESS MATERIALS

C-Record: C-14049

Prepare professional documentation with support from our network of partners in legal, accounting, regulatory, reimbursement, manufacturing, and compliance. Create investor-ready materials including pitch decks, financial models, and regulatory documentation.

Learn More

Module 5: FUNDRAISING-READINESS

C-Record: C-14050

Master investor relations, negotiation strategies, and participate in an online showcase to curated investors and ESOs (Economic Service Organizations). Learn to effectively communicate your value proposition and close funding rounds.

Learn More

Pricing & Reimbursement

Individual Module Pricing

  • Each module: $3,000 (10 hours over 2 weeks)
  • Full program (all 5 modules): $15,000

Express Program Coverage: Eligible employers can receive up to 100% reimbursement through the Commonwealth Corporation's Express Program, making this training essentially free for qualifying businesses.

Process & Eligibility

Express Program Eligibility

  • Businesses with ≤100 MA employees:
    • Up to $300/hour of training
    • Up to $3,000/person per course
    • Up to $15,000/year per employer
    • 100% reimbursement for businesses with ≤50 employees
    • 50% reimbursement for businesses with 51-100 employees
  • Businesses with >100 employees: Eligible for the General Program (different reimbursement structure - contact for details)

Note: All training must be completed within the program year. Contact Commonwealth Corporation or LBV for specific eligibility questions and application assistance.
